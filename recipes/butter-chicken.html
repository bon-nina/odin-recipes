• Butter Chicken •

Description

An easy butter chicken recipe. The sauce is perfect when creamy and not too thick or too thin.
Garam and tandoori masala can be found in the ethnic aisle of your grocery store.

Photo by shibani Mishra on Unsplash

    Ingredients

  • 225g butter
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 450ml tomato sauce
  • 750ml heavy cream
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 650g chicken breast, skinless, boneless
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp tandoori masala

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C.

  2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat.
    Stir in onion and garlic, and cook slowly until the onion caramelizes to a dark brown, about 15 minutes.

  3. Meanwhile, combine cream, tomato sauce, remaining butter, salt, cayenne pepper,
    and garam masala in a saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a simmer.

  4. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes. Stir in caramelized onions.

  5. While the sauce is simmering, toss chicken with vegetable oil until coated.
    Season with tandoori masala and spread out onto a baking sheet.

  6. Bake chicken in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 12 minutes.

  7. Add cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for 5 minutes before serving.
